Week 02- Design Principles

Visual Heirarchy

Apple

Apple.com

Apple uses visual heirarchy to draw your eye exactly where they want it to go. First you read the bold front heading text and then then your eye is drawn to the color elements on the page. Though there are few, their scarcity causes them to stand out.

White Space

Olive Ave Jewelry

Olive Ave Jewelry

This site uses white space to create clean lines, create a cohesive theme, and help the product stand out against the backround.

Hick's Law

Joann

Joann Home Decor Fabric

Because of the vast amount of products on Joann's website, having a clear way to shorten the browsing lists make it a lot easier for customers to find exactly what they are looking for. Hick's Law is followed as we learn that less options usually creates less chances of the customer getting overwhelmed and leaving.
