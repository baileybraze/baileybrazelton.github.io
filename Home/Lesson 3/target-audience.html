Site Plan

Our Target Audience

Our target audience is anyone with access to the internet who enjoys puns and accurate weather data.

Personas

First would be Jane Benin, a 27 year old nurse. She visits the site every morning to see if she can bring her patient outside for a walk or if she needs to plan a different activity.
Second is Milton Shepherd. Milton is 49 and checks the website to see if he needs to cover his garden against the frost. So far, he has not had any plants die.

Senarios

1) Someone looking for the anticipated weather for this week, including wind chill.
2) A mom teaching her children about weather without having to worry about nonsense.
3) An elderly patron looking for easy accesibility and large text options.
4) Classroom and school TV broadcasts of the simple weather.
5) A person looking for ideas on what they can do in the local enviornment.
